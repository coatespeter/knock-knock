This is Takeshi's Castle

The object of the game is simple. Take a run at a door of your choosing. Two of the doors are made of wood (ouch) and one is made of paper. Smash through the paper to make it to the next round. Get lucky 3 times in a row and you are one step closer to storming Takeshi's castle!
